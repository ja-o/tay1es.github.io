How Productiv+ Works

Improving productivity through real time user updates

Productiv+ is an new application to manage and organize team productivity both in a professional and casual environment. Set up team projects, view and start tasks, record time worked, and review project status through daily or weekly progress reports.

Unique to other team management websites, Productiv+ allows you to also view other active team members and what task they are working on. Working in teams, especially across distances, can be unmotivating and lonesome. Just by seeing others work at the same time, Prodcitv+ simulates the boosts in productivity seen while working with a group of people in real life, without distractions of side conversations. Members can also see how many hours each person has contributed to the project in the challenge tab.

GETTING STARTED

  • JOIN/CREATE A PROJECT

    Streamlined interface to not only create projects but also join existing ones. Within projects, various tasks and subtasks can be delegated to teams up to 10 members.

  • RECORD PRODUCTIVITY

    Measure your productivity with a simple timer and compare your hours spent working with your teammates in the competition tab.

  • laptop

    SEE OTHERS WORKING

    Stay motivated by seeing your teammates working on the same or different tasks at the same time as you. Chat features are also availible to coordinate between members.

  • email

    RECIEVE STATUS REPORTS

    Automated emails, sent either daily or weekly, on project status including what work was done, who worked on what, and who worked the most.
