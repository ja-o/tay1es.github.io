Productiv+ is an new application to manage and organize team productivity both in a professional and casual environment. Set up team projects, view and start tasks, record time worked, and review project status through daily progress reports.

Unique to other team management websites, Productiv+ allows you to also view other active team members and what task they are working on. Working in teams, especially across distances, can be unmotivating and lonesome. Just by seeing others work at the same time, Prodcitv+ simulates the boosts in productivity seen when working with a group of people in real life, without distractions of side conversations.
